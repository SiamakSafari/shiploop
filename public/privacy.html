Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Summary: We collect minimal data to provide ShipLoop. We don't sell your data. Your projects and ideas are yours.

1. Information We Collect

Account Info: Email, username, and optional profile details.

Usage Data: Projects, ideas, milestones, and activity you create.

Analytics: Anonymous usage patterns to improve the product.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and improve ShipLoop
  • Calculate your Ship Score and rankings
  • Send important account notifications
  • Process payments (via Stripe)

3. Data Sharing

We don't sell your data. We share only with:

  • Supabase: Database hosting
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Vercel: Hosting

4. Public Features

If you enable a public profile, your username, Ship Score, and ranking may be visible on the leaderboard.

5. Your Rights

  • Export your data anytime
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Opt out of marketing emails

6. Security

We use encryption, secure hosting, and follow industry best practices.
